About me

Hi! I'm Sławek, a React web developer from Poland. I taught myself to code because of boredom, but after doing a few udemy courses I realized I can do many fun and challenging things. Now I'm passionate about code and want to develop in that field.

About two years ago I started learning Python, however 6 months later decided that doing Front End is more intuitive and beginner friendly for me. The transition wasn't that difficult, as many concepts were pretty similar on the basic level, and I'm a pretty quick-witted person.

Aside from programming, you'll often find me immersed in the captivating worlds of fantasy books, where imagination knows no bounds. In my free time I either play computer games or board games with my family.
